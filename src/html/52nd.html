Stiletto

"Stiletto" is a song from Billy Joel's 1978 album 52nd Street. The song is a slow, melancholic ballad with a soaring chorus and a memorable guitar riff. The lyrics describe a romantic relationship that is struggling to survive, with the singer comparing his lover to a stiletto knife - something that is beautiful but also dangerous.
